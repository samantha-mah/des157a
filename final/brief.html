Final Project Brief

Project Summary

The UI/UX and visual design of the Every Picture Project will be improved upon.

Project Description

To improve the UI/UX, I plan to increase the affordance of the site by providing verbal or visual instructions about how to navigate the site. I also plan to increase the visibility of a menu once I build the page to have more content.

To improve the visual design, I have already recieved feedback to delay the onset of the images until after the animation of the camera is triggered to reduce cognitive load, so I plan to implement that. I also want to go out of my comfort zone and experiment with typography.

Expert Feedback

I will conduct a usability test with my current designs and derive key findings to implement for the final project. My users will be other photographers.

The visual design critiques will be conducted with the class undergraduate design tutor and a professional in the industry from my LinkedIn network.
